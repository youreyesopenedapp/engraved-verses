How it works

Simple by design.
Faithful by intention.

A free Scripture flashcard app. No account required — all 66 cards available immediately.

01

Open the Deck

All 66 cards are available immediately — no signup, no barrier. Study in canonical order or shuffle the deck. Filter by Old Testament, New Testament, or memorization status at any time.

02

Flip & Study

The book name and verse reference are on the front. Swipe on mobile or use the arrow keys on desktop to flip the card and reveal the full KJV verse text on the back. Study book-to-verse or verse-to-book.

03

Mark Your Progress

Mark each verse as Still Studying or Memorized. Filter your deck to focus on the verses you're still learning. Watch the Memorized verse count grow with faithful practice.

04

Save Your Place

Enter your email address to save your progress. Progress is saved to this browser and device.
